Welcome to College Of Engineering Attingal Food Ordering Website, the convenient way for our Staffs, Faculty Members and Students to enjoy delicious meals from our campus canteen. Here we prioritize your dining experience, aiming to provide not just nourishment but also convenience and satisfaction.


About the Website

Welcome to our college's dedicated food ordering website, tailored for the convenience of our valued Staffs, Faculty Members and Students. Here, you'll discover a user-friendly platform designed to simplify the process of ordering from our campus canteen. To ensure smooth operations, we kindly request orders to be one day in advance OR before 9 am on the day of pickup, as we currently do not offer online payment options. Our goal is to provide a convenient and efficient experience, allowing you to browse menus, place orders online.we aim to enhance accessibility and ensure that every member of our community can enjoy delicious meals conveniently while on campus.


Key Features and Benefits

  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate our website easily to explore menus, view detailed descriptions and prices, and customize your order.
  • Ordering Flexibility: Place your order by 9 am on the day of pickup or up to one day in advance to ensure prompt service.
  • Customization Options: Personalize your orders with special requests or dietary requirements, ensuring each meal meets your specific needs.
  • Order History: Easily access your past orders for quick reordering or to track your dining preferences over time.
  • Payment Options: Currently, we accept payment upon pickup or delivery, ensuring a hassle-free transaction process.
  • Mobile-Friendly: Access the website on-the-go via mobile devices, making it convenient to place orders from anywhere
  • Community Support: By ordering through our website, you're supporting local vendors and contributing to the sustainability of our campus dining services.
  • Feedback and Improvement: Provide feedback directly through the website, allowing us to continually improve our services and menu offerings based on your input.
